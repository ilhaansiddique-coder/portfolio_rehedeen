## The Operation

RaheDeen's cut-and-sew facility in Narayanganj runs **15 dedicated production lines** staffed by 500+ trained operators. Each line is configured for a specific fabric type — woven, single jersey, French terry, fleece, or ripstop — eliminating the machine changeover delays that slow mixed-fabric factories.

**Machine inventory per line:**
- Single-needle lockstitch (Juki DDL-8700)
- 5-thread overlock (Pegasus)
- Flatlock / coverstitch
- Button-hole, button-attach, bartack
- Blind stitch for hem finishing

## Children's-Specific Construction

Kids' garments demand tighter tolerances and tougher seam integrity than adult apparel. Our standard specifications for children's wear:

- **Seam allowance:** 1.0 cm on body seams, 0.8 cm on cuffs and collars
- **Stitch density:** 12–14 SPI on body seams, 8 SPI on topstitching
- **Flatlock seams** on activewear and base layers to prevent skin irritation
- **Bar-tack reinforcement** at stress points — pocket corners, strap attachments
- **Snap testing:** All snap closures tested per ISO 8317 for infant garments

## MOQ & Flexibility

**300 pieces per style per colour** is our standard MOQ. For styles sharing the same base fabric and construction, we consolidate production runs to help smaller brands access our infrastructure.

For brands ordering 5,000+ pieces per style, dedicated production lines and priority scheduling are available.

## Quality Control

Inline QC is conducted at **20% and 50% of completion** for every order. Our AQL 1.5 final inspection is buyer-auditable. We maintain a 99.4% first-pass rate across all completed orders.
