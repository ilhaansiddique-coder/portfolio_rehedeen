## What Finishing Covers

Finishing is the final production stage — everything that happens after the last stitch and before the carton seal. At RaheDeen, finishing is a dedicated department with its own QC sign-off, not an afterthought on the production floor.

## Garment Washing

Our washing plant handles all standard wet processes for both aesthetic and functional treatment:

- **Stone wash** — Pumice stone abrasion for worn-in denim and canvas finishes
- **Enzyme wash** — Cellulase treatment for bio-polished, soft hand-feel on cotton knits
- **Pigment wash** — Tonal colour shift for overdyed effects on jersey
- **Softener finish** — Silicone or cationic softener application for premium hand-feel

Washing is done in **Dilmenler rotating drum washers** with precise load monitoring. Shrinkage is pre-tested per fabric, and wash programs are validated against buyer spec before bulk.

## Hangtag & Label Attachment

Hangtags are attached per buyer diagram — position, attachment method (safety pin, loop pin, thread bar), and fold orientation. Our in-house hangtag printing means we never wait on a third-party printer.

Label placement is inspected at the QC stage against the approved garment flat spec. Misplaced or crooked labels are the #1 reason for packing rejections — we catch these before packing, not at the buyer's DC.

## Retail-Ready Packing

We pack to **buyer EDI specification** without exception. Our packing team is trained on common retail formats:

- **Fold types:** Flat fold, board fold, hanger pack, polybag with self-adhesive seal
- **Poly bags:** Suffocation warning pre-printed per EN 14682 and ASTM F1553
- **Carton construction:** Buyer dimensions, 5-ply double wall, weight per box maximum
- **Mixed packs:** Size ratio packs pre-confirmed at order stage, scanned per carton

## Barcoding & Scanning

Every garment's barcode is **scanned at the packing station** against the purchase order. Mis-scans trigger an immediate hold — we do not ship un-reconciled cartons. Packing list accuracy rate: 99.97%.

## Final QC

Our pre-shipment inspection follows **AQL 1.5** (Level II sampling). Inspection covers measurements, construction, print/embroidery quality, washing effect, hangtag/label accuracy, and packing conformity. Buyers are welcome to appoint their own 3rd-party inspector (Intertek, Bureau Veritas, SGS, QIMA).
