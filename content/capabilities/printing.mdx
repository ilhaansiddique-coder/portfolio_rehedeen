## Print Processes

### Screen Printing

Our screen printing department handles **up to 8 spot colours** per design using automatic and semi-automatic flatbed presses. Water-based inks are our standard — they maintain softhand feel on jersey and are compatible with OEKO-TEX and EU REACH requirements out of the box.

- **Fabric compatibility:** Cotton, cotton-poly blends, jersey, fleece
- **Print area:** Up to 400mm × 500mm chest; full back; sleeve panels
- **Special effects:** Puff print, discharge, high-density (simulated 3D), shimmer/metallic
- **Ink system:** Rutland water-based (phthalate-free, lead-free, formaldehyde-free)

### All-Over Sublimation

For polyester-based garments, we run **continuous sublimation printing** directly onto fabric before cutting. This eliminates print registration issues and allows photographic-quality, edge-to-edge coverage.

- **Fabric compatibility:** Minimum 80% polyester required for proper dye migration
- **Resolution:** Up to 1440 DPI
- **Colours:** Unlimited — CMYK process
- **Lead time:** 5 days fabric print + standard production schedule

### DTG (Direct to Garment)

For small runs, sampling, or photographic artwork on dark garments, our **DTG units** print directly onto finished garments. No minimum order — ideal for fit samples with final print, or limited-edition drops.

- **Fabric compatibility:** 100% cotton preferred; blends accepted
- **Resolution:** 1200 DPI
- **White ink base coat** available for dark fabric printing
- **MOQ:** 1 piece (sample), 50 pieces (commercial run)

### Heat Transfer Vinyl

For name-and-number kits, sport programme orders, and individualised branding, our **HTV cutting plotters** produce precise vinyl transfers for heat application. Common for school uniform and team kit programmes.

## Ink Compliance

All inks and dyes used across our print processes are:

- **Phthalate-free** (compliant with EU REACH Annex XVII, entry 51)
- **Lead and heavy metal free** (tested per EN 71-3)
- **Formaldehyde-free** (below detection threshold on Raman testing)
- **Azo dye-free** (no carcinogenic amines per EU Directive 2002/61/EC)

Test certificates from SGS and Intertek available per print process on request.

## MOQ

Screen printing: **300 pieces** per design per colourway. Sublimation: **500m fabric minimum** (approximately 300–500 garments depending on yield). DTG: no minimum for samples, 50 pieces for commercial runs.
