## What Full Package Means at RaheDeen

When a brand comes to us for private label or OEM, we act as their **single-point vendor** — removing the coordination burden of managing separate design, sourcing, production, and logistics suppliers.

The full package service covers:

1. **Design development** — Translate your brief, mood board, or reference garment into a construction-ready tech pack. Our in-house design team works to your brand's aesthetic.
2. **Pattern grading** — Grade from your nominated base size across your full size range, with measurement charts per grade.
3. **Fabric + trim sourcing** — Access to GOTS-certified and OEKO-TEX compliant mills. We place bulk fabric orders, negotiate mill minimums, and warehouse surplus.
4. **Bulk production** — On our dedicated production lines with weekly WIP reporting.
5. **Lab testing** — 3rd-party testing through SGS or Bureau Veritas. Test reports provided per style.
6. **Branding + labelling** — In-house hangtag printing, woven label sourcing, care label compliance (EU/US/AU regulation), barcode generation and scanning.
7. **Retail-ready packing** — Per your EDI specification: poly bag folding, carton construction, ticketing, mixed-size packs.

## In-House Branding Capabilities

We manage your brand touchpoints without third-party printers or label agents:

- **Woven labels** — Sourced from certified mills; neck labels, size labels, care labels
- **Hangtags** — Printed in-house, up to 6 colours, with barcode, price point, and care symbol
- **Barcodes** — EAN-13 and UPC-A, scanned and verified at packing
- **Polybag printing** — Suffocation warnings per EN 14682 and ASTM F1553 pre-printed on bags

## Why Brands Choose Full Package Over FOB

For brands without internal sourcing teams, full package reduces risk. You sign off at two points — sample approval and bulk shipment inspection — rather than managing 8–12 individual vendors.

For brands with existing sourcing teams, our OEM service provides surge capacity without the overhead of a local team.

## Minimums

Full package orders start at **500 pieces per style**. For design development only (no production commitment), a development fee applies and is refunded in full when you place a bulk order.
