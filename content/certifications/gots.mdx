## What GOTS Certifies

**The Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS)** is the world's leading processing standard for textiles made from organic fibres. Unlike fibre-only organic standards, GOTS covers the **entire textile processing chain** — fibre, spinning, knitting, dyeing, finishing, and garment manufacturing.

### Organic Content Requirements

| Label Claim | Organic Fibre Minimum | Synthetic Fibre Maximum |
|---|---|---|
| **"Organic"** | ≥95% | ≤5% |
| **"Made with X% Organic"** | ≥70% | ≤30% |

For our standard GOTS organic cotton garments, we work exclusively with fabrics that meet the **"Organic"** label criteria (≥95% certified organic cotton).

## Ecological Processing Criteria

GOTS doesn't just certify the fibre — it certifies the processing:

- **Prohibited substances:** All substances on the GOTS prohibited input list (including formaldehyde, heavy metals in dyes, azo dyes cleaving to carcinogenic amines)
- **Dyes and auxiliaries:** Must be GOTS-approved. Our mill partners use certified reactive dyes with formaldehyde-free fixative
- **Wastewater treatment:** GOTS-certified mills must operate an approved wastewater treatment plant. Our Gazipur mill partner is GOTS-audited annually

## Social Criteria

GOTS incorporates ILO labour conventions:

- No forced labour, no child labour
- Freedom of association and collective bargaining
- Safe and healthy working conditions
- Living wage benchmarked annually

Our facility is audited against these criteria as part of our GOTS re-certification cycle.

## Chain of Custody

The GOTS chain of custody works through **transaction certificates (TCs)**. Every transfer of certified goods — mill to garment factory, garment factory to buyer — is documented with a TC that references the specific fabric lot, weight, and certification number.

RaheDeen issues TCs for all GOTS organic garment orders. Buyers receive the TC alongside the shipment, enabling them to maintain their own chain-of-custody documentation for retail claims.
