## What EU REACH Is

**REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals)** is the European Union's primary chemicals regulation, enforced since 2007. For textile importers, REACH restricts the use of specific hazardous substances in garments and accessories sold in the EU market.

Non-compliance is not a paperwork issue — it triggers product recalls, market bans, and significant liability for importers.

## SVHC List — Substances of Very High Concern

The REACH **Substances of Very High Concern (SVHC)** list is updated twice yearly by ECHA. Current textile-relevant SVHC entries include:

- Certain phthalate plasticisers (DEHP, DBP, BBP, DIBP) — present in PVC-coated trims
- Nonylphenol and nonylphenol ethoxylates (NPE) — found in some processing auxiliaries
- Chromium VI compounds — from certain leather or chrome-tanned trims
- Short-chain chlorinated paraffins (SCCPs) — present in some flame retardants

RaheDeen tests all finished garments and relevant trims against the **current SVHC candidate list** annually, using SGS Dhaka and Intertek Bangladesh.

## Restricted Substances in Practice

**Azo dyes:** EU Directive 2002/61/EC restricts azo dyes that cleave to form any of 22 carcinogenic amines. All dyes used at our mill partners are screened against this list. Garment-level azo dye testing is conducted by Intertek per colour per season.

**Phthalates:** Restricted under REACH Annex XVII, entry 51. No phthalate-containing PVC is used in any RaheDeen garment component. All print inks are water-based and certified phthalate-free.

**Formaldehyde:** Not restricted under REACH per se, but restricted under individual EU member state regulations (notably Germany's Bedarfsgegenständeverordnung). We apply the strictest member-state limit (≤75ppm for Class II; ≤30ppm for Class I baby items) as our internal standard.

## Testing Schedule

| Frequency | Test Scope | Lab |
|---|---|---|
| Per season | Finished garment SVHC screen | SGS Dhaka |
| Per colour | Azo dye analysis | Intertek Bangladesh |
| Per new ink/dye introduction | Full REACH screen | SGS or Bureau Veritas |
| Annual | Full print/dye supplier audit | Internal + 3rd party |

## Providing REACH Documentation to Buyers

Our REACH test reports are available to buyers on request, per style or per season. EU-destined shipments include a REACH compliance statement in the documentation package.
