## Range Overview

Our boys' range covers **40+ base styles** from newborn through 14 years. Every style is available in our standard size grading or can be adapted to buyer-specific size charts.

### Core Styles

**Tops**
- Short-sleeve T-shirt (crew neck, V-neck, polo)
- Long-sleeve T-shirt
- Raglan sweatshirt
- Pullover hoodie (kangaroo pocket, fleece-lined)
- Zip-through hoodie

**Bottoms**
- Jogger pants (ribbed cuff, woven drawstring)
- Cargo pants (functional pockets, twill)
- Denim shorts and pants
- Track pants with side stripe

**Sets & Outerwear**
- 2-piece jersey sets (top + jogger)
- 3-piece sets (body + top + bottoms)
- Puffer jacket (lightweight, packable)
- Softshell jacket (windproof, fleece-backed)
- Rain jacket (seam-sealed, PU coated)

| Category | Fabric | GSM Range |

| Basic T-shirts | 100% cotton single jersey | 150–180 |

All cotton is available in **GOTS-certified organic** on request.

## Construction Details

- Flatlock seams on activewear to prevent friction on sensitive skin
- Reinforced double-needle hem on T-shirts
- Elastic waistbands: covered in matching fabric (not exposed elastic)
- **No drawstrings at hood or neckline** on garments sized 0–7 years (EN 14682 compliant)
- Functional pockets on cargo styles — depth tested against choking hazard items

## Compliance

All boys' garments are produced to EU and US safety standards. CPSIA documentation (GCC + 3rd-party test reports) available for USA-destined orders. OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified fabrics available on request.
