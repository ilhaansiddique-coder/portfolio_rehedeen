## Range Overview

Our girls' range covers **50+ base styles** from newborn through 14 years, with a broad mix of woven and jersey constructions. Design details — shirring, smocking, lace trim, ruffle panels — are built into our standard offerings, not add-on services.

### Core Styles

**Tops & Blouses**
- Short-sleeve blouse (cotton voile, viscose)
- Ruffle-sleeve top (jersey)
- Puff-sleeve blouse (woven)
- Crop top (for 10–14y, jersey)
- Long-sleeve thermal top

**Dresses & Skirts**
- A-line dress (woven + jersey options)
- Smocked bodice dress
- Tiered ruffle dress
- Pinafore dress (denim or cotton twill)
- Tutu skirt (tulle over jersey base)
- Midi skirt (jersey, A-line)

**Bottoms**
- Leggings (full length, 7/8, capri)
- Flared pants (jersey)
- Wide-leg trousers (woven)
- Shorts (jersey + woven)

**Sets & Activewear**
- 2-piece activewear sets (sports bra + legging)
- 3-piece jersey sets
- Matching co-ord sets (printed)

## Design Details We Produce In-House

Shirring (elastic thread smocking), French seam construction for unlined woven dresses, interlining in bodices for structured fit, bound buttonholes on woven styles — all executed by our specialist sewing stations.

## Compliance

No drawstring closures on sizes 0–7y. Functional cord limitations on hood openings. All lace and trim tested for formaldehyde content (below 75ppm per EU standard). CPSIA and EU EN 14682 documentation available.
