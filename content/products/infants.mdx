## The Compliance-First Age Group

Infant garments (0–12 months) are the most regulated category in children's apparel. Every design decision — from snap placement to dye selection — has a compliance implication. Our infant range is built compliance-first, with testing documentation included as standard.

## Core Styles

**One-Pieces**
- Short-sleeve bodysuit (snap crotch)
- Long-sleeve bodysuit
- Sleepsuit / footie pajama (2-way zip or snap down front)
- Sleep sack (wearable blanket, sleeveless)

**Separates**
- Knit pant (elasticated waist, no drawstring)
- Short-sleeve top
- Cardigan (front snap closure)

**Accessories**
- Reversible bib (bandana and standard)
- Knotted hat
- Booties with ankle snap
- 3-piece gift sets (bodysuit + pant + hat)

## Fabric Standards

**Standard fabric:** 100% combed cotton single jersey, 160–180 GSM
**Premium option:** GOTS-certified organic cotton (same construction, certified supply chain)

Both options are available with **formaldehyde-free reactive dyes**, tested to below 20ppm — the EU standard for baby products is 75ppm; we build in a significant safety margin.

## Construction Specifics for Infant Safety

- **Snap closures:** All metal snaps tested per ISO 8317 for attachment force and cycle life. We do not use magnetic closures on infant garments.
- **No drawstrings or cords** — category prohibition for 0–24 months under EN 14682
- **No decorative buttons under 31.75mm** on garments 0–36 months — choking hazard per ASTM F963
- **Flatlock seaming on all body seams** — eliminates internal ridge against delicate skin
- **2-way zips on sleepsuits** — allows nappy access from the bottom without full undress
- **Footie construction:** Non-slip textured finish on soles for early walkers (12m+)

## CPSIA Documentation for USA Market

For USA-destined infant orders, we provide per style:

- **General Conformity Certificate (GCC)** signed by our compliance manager
- **3rd-party test report** from SGS or Bureau Veritas covering lead content, phthalates, and flammability (16 CFR 1615/1616 for sleepwear)
- **Tracking label** on every finished garment with style number, factory address, and date of manufacture
