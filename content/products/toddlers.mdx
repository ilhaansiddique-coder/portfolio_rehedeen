## The Toddler Design Brief

Toddlers (1–3 years) are active, messy, and growing fast. The design brief for this category prioritises four things: **comfort** (no itchy seams or tight waistbands), **durability** (repeated washing at high temperature), **easy-dress** (parents need to get these on and off quickly), and **safety** (no small parts, no cords, no choking hazards).

Every style in our toddler range is tested for all four criteria before leaving sampling.

## Construction Details

**Elastic waistbands:** All waistbands use covered elastic (fabric sleeve over elastic) at a tension appropriate for toddler body shape. Exposed elastic contracts and can pinch.

**Low-pill fleece:** Our standard toddler fleece is tested for pilling resistance to **ISO 12945-2** (Martindale, 10,000 rubs) before use in bulk. Pilling is the #1 consumer complaint for toddler knitwear.

**Snap-close bib overalls:** Inseam snaps on all-in-one styles allow full access without undressing — critical for nappy changes. Snap attachment tested per ISO 8317.

**Machine washability:** All fabrics and trims for this category are pre-tested at **60°C, 40 wash cycles** against IEC 456. Shrinkage spec is ±3% on length and width. Colour fastness to washing tested to ISO 105-C06.

## Sizing

Standard EU sizing (68/74/80/86/92/98). UK, US, or buyer-specific sizing available. We do not auto-convert between sizing systems — buyer-approved measurement charts are mandatory before sampling.

## Safety Standards

- No drawstrings or cords on garments 0–7 years (EN 14682)
- All decorative trims tested for detachment force per EN 71-1 (choking prevention)
- Fabric tested for formaldehyde: ≤75ppm (EU standard for Class II textile next to skin)
- All printing inks: phthalate-free, azo dye-free, REACH compliant
